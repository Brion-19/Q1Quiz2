My Favorite Animated Movie: Ratatouille

    Ratatouille is a movie about a rat named Remy who dreams to be a chef. He was able to get the help of a garbage boy named Linguini and show his culinary skills in the kitchen by hiding in a chef hat. In the end Remy was given a chance to provie his amazing culinary skills and impress a famous food critic dispite being a rat.


The characters:



Remy (Patton Oswalt)


Alfredo Linguini (Lou Romano)


Anton Ego (Peter O'Toole)


Skinner (Ian Holm)


Colette Tatou (Janeane Garofalo)
